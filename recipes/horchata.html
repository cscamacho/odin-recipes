Horchata Recipe

Horchata is a traditional Mexican drink typically made from white rice. This is a common drink you will find in markets, restaurants, and homes.

Horchata de arroz is commonly found in Mexico and Guatemala and has become popular in taquerias in the United States. Though horchata was typically homemade, it is now avaialble in both ready-to-drink and powdered form in grocery stores.

Horchata is one of the three typical drink flavors of Mexican aguas frescas, alongside tamarindo and jamaica.

Ingredients Include:

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Cinammon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Soak rice overnight in a bucket of water.
  2. Throw out rice in the morning, keeping the water.
  3. Add water, milk, sugar, and cinnamon to blender.
  4. Blend all ingredients and then refrigerate.
